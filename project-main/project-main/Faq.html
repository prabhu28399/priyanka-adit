FAQ'S

Frequently Asked Questions on Grievance Redress Mechanism in Government of India and the Role of Department of Administrative Reforms and Public Grievance, New Delhi

Our platform aims to ensure that government schemes reach every eligible individual in India. We address issues such as corruption, lack of information, and intermediaries who may exploit or mislead applicants.

You can report issues or grievances directly on our platform. Alternatively, you can also send your grievance to the respective government department handling the scheme via email or post. For further assistance, contact our support team..

You can report issues such as:

  • Demands for bribes or illegal payments
  • Lack of proper information or guidance on scheme applications
  • Corruption or malpractice by intermediaries
  • Any other problems faced while accessing government schemes

To submit a grievance, fill out the online form on our website. If you prefer, you can also send a written complaint via email or post. For issues requiring immediate attention, visit our help center or contact us directly....

Once your grievance is submitted, you will receive an acknowledgment with a unique reference number. Our team will review and forward your complaint to the relevant authorities for resolution.

You can track the status of your grievance on our platform using the unique reference number provided to you. Regular updates will be available on your dashboard....

Grievances are forwarded to the relevant government departments or officials responsible for the specific scheme. We monitor the progress and ensure timely redressal...

If you are not satisfied with the resolution, you will need to submit a new grievance, referencing the previous one. Provide details of the unsatisfactory resolution for further review.

If you encounter problems accessing government schemes, you can report these issues on our platform. We will assist you in navigating the process and ensure your concerns are addressed by the relevant authorities..

We do not handle grievances related to:

  • Ongoing legal proceedings or court judgments
  • Personal and family disputes
  • Right to Information (RTI) matters
  • Issues affecting national security or international relations

We collaborate with government departments to ensure grievances are addressed effectively. Our platform acts as an intermediary, facilitating communication between citizens and the authorities to expedite the resolution process.

The standard time frame for resolving grievances is 30 days. In case of delays, an interim update will be provided explaining the reasons for the delay.

If your grievance is not resolved within 30 days, you can escalate the matter by contacting our support team or the relevant government department. We will assist in ensuring your concern is addressed promptly.

You have the option to appeal the decision. An appeal provision is available for cases where you are not satisfied with the resolution provided by the government department. Ensure you provide feedback within the stipulated time frame.

To deactivate your account, send a request to our support team via email. The email should be sent from the registered email address. Once deactivated, you will not be able to use the same email address for creating a new account without reactivation.

If you need to update your details or those of the officials, log in to your account and make the necessary changes through the user settings. For assistance, contact our support team.

Yes, we have a feedback system in place to collect your opinions on the resolution of grievances. Your feedback helps us improve the service and address any issues effectively.
